---
title: Disable old notifications balloons
slug: related/notifications
description: How to disable the old notification balloons?
sidebar_position: 14
sidebar_class_name: scrape-me-daddy
---

1. Open `regedit`
2. Go to `HKEY_CURRENT_USER\SOFTWARE\Policies\Microsoft\Windows\Explorer`
3. Set `EnableLegacyBalloonNotifications` to `0`
